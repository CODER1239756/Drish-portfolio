About Drish

Builder. Creator. Athlete.

Drish Shah is someone who moves between worlds without losing himself in any of them. One moment he is pushing code, the next he is behind a lens finding the light that everyone else walks past.

"He builds the things he cannot find and photographs the things everyone else forgets to look at."

Born on the 24th of September, he brings that same autumn-evening energy to everything — unhurried, thoughtful, and unexpectedly beautiful.
